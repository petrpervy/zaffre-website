Frequently asked questions.

How can you deliver quality work in 1-2 weeks?

We focus on execution, not endless meetings. Fixed packages mean we know exactly what to build. No bureaucracy, no approval chains—just three developers working efficiently on your project. Check our portfolio to see the results.

Why are your prices lower than big agencies?

Small team, zero overhead. No office rent, account managers, or sales team to pay for. We're three developers who work directly with clients—so you get professional quality without agency markup. Simple as that.

Do I own the code and design?

Yes. Once the project is complete and paid, you own everything. All code, designs, and assets are transferred to you. No licensing fees, no ongoing dependencies.

What if I need changes after launch?

All packages include 2 weeks of post-launch support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. For ongoing updates, we offer maintenance plans starting at $50/month.

Can you integrate with my existing systems?

Yes. We integrate with CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), scheduling systems (Calendly, Acuity), payment processors (Stripe, Square), and most other business tools via API.
